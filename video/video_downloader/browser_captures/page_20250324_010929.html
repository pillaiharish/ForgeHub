Frequently asked questions

Can’t find it here? Check out our Help Center.

  • What is an HLS/m3u8 player?

    An HLS/M3U8 player is a type of player specifically designed to support HTTP Live Streaming (HLS) and M3U8 playlist files. HLS/m3u8 players are essential for delivering high-quality, adaptive video streams across different devices, ensuring a seamless viewing experience across all devices.

  • What is the M3U8 link?

    An M3U8 link is a URL or file path that points to a playlist file used in HTTP Live Streaming (HLS). This file format, which ends in ".m3u8", contains a list of media files, or segments, that are part of a multimedia presentation. It is particularly used for delivering streaming video content over the internet and is widely supported across various devices and platforms.

  • How to test an m3u8 link?

    Testing an M3U8 link to ensure smooth streaming quality is straightforward with Castr HLS/m3u8 player. Follow these steps to test your HLS streams effectively:
    1. Go to castr.com/hlsplayer to access the player
    2. Enter the m3u8 link you wish to test into the designated field and click “Play” to initiate the stream.
    3. Castr player will load and and begin playing the stream.
    4. You can now watch the playback to assess video quality and stability. Look for any issues such as buffering, interruptions, or quality degradation.

  • How does Adaptive Bitrate Streaming improve viewing experience?

    Adaptive Bitrate Streaming dynamically adjusts video quality during playback based on the viewer's network conditions. This feature minimizes buffering, lag, or delays, ensuring an optimal viewing experience even when internet speeds fluctuate.

  • How can I ensure the security of my HLS streams?

    Our HLS/M3U8 player offers several security features, including password protection, GEO blocking, and domain referrer protection. These features help control access to your streams, ensuring that your content is viewed only by your intended audience and is protected against unauthorized use.

  • What is Castr?

    Castr is a video streaming platform that specializes in providing top-tier solutions for livestreaming, multistreaming, video-on-demand (VOD), and IP camera streaming solutions.

  • What types of subscription plans does Castr offer?

    Castr offer several subscription plans that meet every need. From low cost plans to advanced and enterprise-level solutions. Check out Castr pricing for more information. All plans come with an embeddable player powered by Akamai CDN, ensuring optimal HLS stream delivery. Features also include multistreaming, video hosting, and integrated paywall features.
